# 🎵 Beginner Pieces (Level 1)

*Use open strings and fingers 1-3. Simple rhythms. One string at a time.*

**How to read the codes:** `A1` = A string, 1st finger. `E0` = E string, open. `D3` = D string, 3rd finger.

---

**Key:** No sharps/flats | **Time:** 4/4

  Finger: 1    0    3         1    0    3

## Piece 3: Twinkle Twinkle Little Star

**Key:** D Major (F#, C#) | **Time:** 4/4 | **Strings:** A + E

This is the famous **Suzuki Book 1, #1**!

```
  Section A:
  Note:   A    A    E    E  | F#   F#   E        |
  Finger: 0    0    0    0  | 1    1    0        |
  String: A    A    E    E  | E    E    E (half) |
  Code:   A0   A0   E0   E0| E1   E1   E0       |
  Count:  1    2    3    4  | 1    2    3    4   |

  Note:   D    D    C#   C# | B    B    A        |
  Finger: 3    3    2    2  | 1    1    0        |
  String: A    A    A    A  | A    A    A (half) |
  Code:   A3   A3   A2   A2| A1   A1   A0       |

  Section B (x2):
  Note:   E    E    D    D  | C#   C#   B        |
  Finger: 0    0    3    3  | 2    2    1        |
  String: E    E    A    A  | A    A    A (half) |
  Code:   E0   E0   A3   A3| A2   A2   A1       |

  Then repeat Section A.
```

## 🎯 Practice Order

```
  Week 1-2:  Hot Cross Buns → Mary Had a Little Lamb
  Week 3-4:  Twinkle Twinkle → Lightly Row
  Week 5-6:  Go Tell Aunt Rhody → Ode to Joy
```

> **Rule:** Don't move to the next piece until you can play the current one
> **3 times in a row without mistakes** at a slow tempo.
